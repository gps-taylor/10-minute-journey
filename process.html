The process

In 10 minutes,
come into alignment.

Come into presence. Clear the noise. Align with what matters. A conscious way of living.

Intentional self-alignment is design — a method, a way of being. When one person aligns, the world around them begins to shift.

Building the Foundation
01
Start here

Begin in Embodied Presence

The 10 Minute Journey to You begins by strengthening your embodied presence — the felt experience of being fully grounded, aware, and in ongoing communication with yourself.

This presence is how you listen to the signals of your body, emotions, and environment. It reveals what supports you, what drains you, and what needs your care. As you build a consistent relationship with this internal guidance, it becomes the lens for intentional action and aligned choices.

This is where the journey starts, where it builds, and where you connect again and again.

02
Clear the noise

Do the Housekeeping

Housekeeping is the process of recognizing the comparisons, expectations, old narratives, and external pressures that shape your thoughts, feelings, and choices.

As they become visible, you can begin to ask whether they truly align with who you are and what matters most to you. Some may stay. Others may be ready to be cleared away, making room for deeper alignment.

03
What emerges

Move to your Areas of Importance

What emerges from presence are your Areas of Importance — deeply felt priorities that may resemble values, goals, or inner calls. They are shaped by your presence, your lived experience, and the season of life you are in now.

These are not fixed, and they are not a prescriptive narrative. They are what you find most important to your core. Your 10-minute sessions in presence work to facilitate and unveil them, bringing you closer to you.

Living the Practice
04
The journey in 10

Journey to You

Once your Areas of Importance come into focus, with curiosity, you move through phases of inquiry, experimentation, and integration — Research and Discovery, Implementation, and Steady-self — while completing your 10-minute sessions. Throughout, you return to key Transition Points for Reflection and applicable Adjustments.

This journey is both structured and fluid, providing form without rigidity. It is about understanding yourself through a rhythm of intentional action and embodied connection — not chasing outcomes or perfecting routines. With each return to presence, you deepen your alignment, allowing your life to unfold with more clarity, authenticity, and ease.

The three phases

A flexible progression you move through, return to, and refine.

Phase 01

Research & Discovery

Explore what resonates. Move with curiosity through your uniquely identified Areas of Importance.

Phase 02

Implementation

Engage through 10-minute activities rooted in intentionality, testing what may truly fulfill you.

Phase 03

Steady-self

Settle into alignment. Return to key transition points for reflection and gentle adjustment.

The 10 Minute Journey to You — never linear.
